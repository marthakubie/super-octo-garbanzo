    About Us

    We are a non-profit organization dedicated to helping animals in developing countries. Our mission is to build shelters and provide vaccinations to homeless and abandoned animals, and to find them loving and permanent homes.

    1. Our values:
      • Compassion
      • Respect
      • Responsibility
      • Integrity
    2. Our goals:
      • To reduce animal suffering
      • To improve animal welfare in developing countries
      • To promote responsible pet ownership

    Our Services

    We provide the following services:

    • Building shelters for homeless animals
    • Providing vaccinations to prevent diseases
    • Spaying and neutering to control animal populations
    • Rescuing and rehabilitating injured animals
